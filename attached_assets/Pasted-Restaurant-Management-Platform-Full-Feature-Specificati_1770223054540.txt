Restaurant Management Platform – Full Feature Specification (Developer Guide)
0. General Principles

This system is a multi-tenant SaaS restaurant platform designed to support single or multi-branch restaurants. It must be stable, scalable, bilingual (Arabic RTL + English LTR), and compliant with Saudi regulations (ZATCA, Mada).

Core Non‑Functional Requirements

Smooth UX, no blocking operations

Fully bilingual (content + UI)

RTL/LTR switch without reload

Real‑time updates where required

Offline tolerance for POS

Secure, auditable, fault‑tolerant

1. Restaurant & Branch Management
1.1 Restaurant Entity

Each restaurant is a top‑level tenant.

Fields:

Name (AR / EN)

Address

Phone, Email

Kitchen Type (enum)

Price Range (enum)

Working Hours (per day)

Logo, Banner

Theme settings (colors, layout)

Rules:

One owner per restaurant

Global settings inherited by branches unless overridden

1.2 Branches

Restaurants may have multiple branches.

Branch Fields:

Location (lat/lng + address)

Contact info

Branch manager

Enabled services (Dine‑in, Pickup, Delivery)

Behavior:

Independent orders, inventory, tables

Unified reporting at restaurant level
